Government Schemes for Farmers

1. Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana

PM-KISAN is a flagship scheme of the Government of India, under which income support of Rs. 6,000 per year is provided to eligible farmer families, payable in three equal installments of Rs. 2,000 each. The scheme aims to provide financial assistance to small and marginal farmers for purchasing inputs like seeds, fertilizers, and equipment.

2. Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

The Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) promotes organic farming, aiming to enhance the quality of soil, water, and air. It encourages farmers to adopt eco-friendly and sustainable farming practices while also providing financial assistance for organic certification, training, and marketing of organic produce.

3. National e-Governance Plan in Agriculture (NeGPA)

The NeGPA aims to promote the use of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in the agricultural sector. The scheme focuses on building a digital infrastructure for farmers, providing them with weather forecasting, market prices, weather alerts, and e-agriculture solutions to enhance productivity and reduce losses.

4. Radical Area Development (RAD)

The Radical Area Development (RAD) scheme is designed to promote farming in areas that are less developed or have been impacted by droughts, floods, or other challenges. The scheme offers financial assistance for infrastructure development, irrigation facilities, and technological upgrades to improve agricultural productivity.

5. Agriculture Infrastructure Fund

The Agriculture Infrastructure Fund aims to provide funding for the creation of necessary infrastructure in the agriculture sector.
